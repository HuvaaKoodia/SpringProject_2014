-Room XML attributes-
loot_a=target amount of loot crates in this room (min,max)
enemy_a=target amount of enemies in this room (min,max)

-Room Legend-
, = space
. = floor
w = wall
d = door
h = elevator

(Further object specifications in ShipGenerator.xml)
 
-Ship XML attributes-
loot_a=target amount of loot items in this ship floor (min,max)
enemy_a=target amount of enemies in this ship floor (min,max)

-Ship Legend- (all tiles in Room Legend usable aswell)
c = corridor
r = room space
R = room start (top left corner) 
T = room end (use if rooms right next to each other)

(Further room indices specified in ShipGenerator.xml)
